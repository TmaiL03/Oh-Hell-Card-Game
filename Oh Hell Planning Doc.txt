Oh Hell Card Game:

Scenes:
- Card
	- Has suit and value.
- Player
	- Has set of cards representing player's hand, is dealt each turn.
- Deck
	- Stores list of 52 cards.
	- shuffle()
	- deal()
- GameManager
	- Stores list of players.
	- Handles game state (bidding, playing rounds, scoring)
	- Deals cards to players.

Scoring:
Correct Bid = 0 => 5pts
Correct Bid >0 => 10 + number of tricks correctly bid.

Game Flow:
- Bidding
	- Every player makes a bid
- Play Tricks
	- In order, player leads with a card of their choice, all subsequent players must play a card of the same suit if possible, if not, they are free to play any card they would like.
- Scoring
	- Once all players have played one card each, the player with the highest value card played wins the trick.

Repeat until all cards have been played.
